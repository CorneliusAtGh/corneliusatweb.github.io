If you can't download the file for whatever reason, try again later. If you exceed the maximum download limit, contact me to reset it. Learn more.


Q: How can I get a refund?

A: You can only get a refund within 5 business days from making the purchase under the condition that you DO NOT download the content beforehand. If those two conditions are met, contact me through the above form and request your refund.


Q: What if I can't download my purchased item(s)?

A: Contact me through the above form, and we'll see if we can resolve the issue. If not, a refund is in order.


Q: Do I receive updates to my purchased content?

A: It all depends on whether your purchased content will be receiving updates in the first place. If it will, then yes. If you have used up your download limit, contact me through the above form to reset it. If you lost the download link, also contact me through the same above-mentioned form, and I will issue you your link anew.
