§ I: Data & Privacy

As for data retention, I, personally, only keep your data until your inquiry has been addressed (*). This may not, however, apply to FormSubmit, whose service I use to receive your message(s), and Google, whose mail client I use to view and respond to your message(s). I highly advise going through their legal documents in order to learn how they handle your data.


Additionally, I may occasionally feature links, embeds, or the like of some third-party services, such as YouTube, TikTok, Bluesky, &c. As such, I highly advise, again, going through their legal documents to learn how they specifically handle your data.


And I will never, under any circumstance, share your data with any third parties, except when required by law or for legal reasons.


(*) If you are using me as a publisher of your work, I retain your name or username, per your preference, which can be found on your work's dedicated page, alongside your email, which is privately kept in my Google mail client. In this case, your data is stored indefinitely or until you request the content to be removed. The reason behind this is so the other users know exactly who made the content they are looking at, and so they can adequately attribute it to you.
