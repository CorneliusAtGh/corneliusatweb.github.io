>> 1863


Alongside writing a book, I also always wanted to make a game. Over the years, I have played around in many a game engine, everything from Unreal Engine to CryEngine, but I ultimately settled on Godot. My game is set in an alternate universe where the United States of America merged with the United Kingdom (alongside all their territories, colonies, &c.) to form the State in 1863. The exact time in which my game itself is set specifically is not defined; however, you do assume the role of a Colonel tasked with defeating the State's enemies no matter the cost. It will be a relatively simple 2D top-down strategy game.
